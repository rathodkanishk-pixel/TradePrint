Import from Broker
Export your trade history from your broker as a CSV file and import it here. TradePrint automatically reads your broker's format — no reformatting needed.
Important: Imported trades will still need you to add emotion, setup type, and rule adherence — this data only exists in your head. After importing, go to Trade History and review each trade to add these details for accurate edge analysis.

How to export from Zerodha
1
Go to console.zerodha.com
2
Click Reports in the top menu
3
Click Tradebook
4
Select your date range (up to 1 year at a time)
5
Click Download CSV — upload that file below
How to export from Dhan
1
Log in to web.dhan.co
2
Click your Profile icon → Statement & Reports
3
Select All Exchange Transactions
4
Choose your date range and click Download as CSV
5
Upload that file below
How to export from Upstox
1
Log in to upstox.com
2
Go to Reports → Tradebook
3
Select your date range
4
Click Download → choose CSV
5
Upload that file below
